Building Dreams Since 2002

Our Founder Mr M.Ponmozhi selvan began his carreer as an Engineer in R.M Constructions.

Quality is never an accident; it is always the result of high intention, sincere effort, intelligent direction, and skillful execution. - John Ruskin

With 10+ successful projects and 13 years of experience, he dreamt of establishing a construction company that would not only build structures but also create lifelong relationships and enduring legacies.

From its humble beginnings, PMR Builder set out on a remarkable journey of growth and excellence. Starting with a small team of dedicated professionals, the company quickly gained recognition for its commitment to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction.

With each project undertaken, the company strived to exceed expectations, delivering impeccable craftsmanship and attention to detail. From residential homes to commercial developments, PMR Builder's portfolio began to showcase an impressive range of successful projects.

PMR Builder's unwavering commitment to excellence and its dedication to customer satisfaction led to the establishment of long-lasting relationships with clients, architects, and industry partners. The company's reputation for integrity, reliability, and transparency became its guiding principles.

Today, with over two decades of experience, PMR Builder stands tall as a respected name in the construction industry. Its commitment to quality, innovation, and client-centricity remains unwavering. The company continues to build remarkable structures that blend aesthetics with functionality, creating spaces that inspire and uplift.

With each new project, it continues to shape the future of the construction industry, leaving an indelible mark on the world, one building at a time.
